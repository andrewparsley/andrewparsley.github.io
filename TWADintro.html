Welcome to T.W.A.D.!!!

heres how to play!

read the story, then read your two options, A and B.

choose which one you like best. this is the choice your character will make in the game.

click one of the two buttons, A or B.

and repeat! each time you make a choice, the story will move in a new direction.

when you get to the end of a path, the game will prompt you to write a new chapter describing what happens next.

your chapter can be anything you choose. switch genres, write short, write long, write dark, write funny. continue the story however you see fit.

fill in all the fields with your chapter, and press the Send Message! button.

once you have sent your new chapter, refresh the page. your chapter will be added to the game, where others can reply to it.

play the game many times, exploring different paths each time. check in on your chapters to see if others have written what comes next.

have fun!
